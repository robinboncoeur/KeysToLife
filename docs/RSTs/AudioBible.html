Introduction

Why These Pages?

Des Ford’s talks - talks I would save to .mp3 and listen to as I headed for work - have led me back to Scripture and the Good News, particularly the writings of Paul, who lays out why we believe in the Good News. I thought it might be a good idea to actually listen to Paul’s writings themselves.

I have also “audified” devotional PDFs by Dr Ford. The voice, obviously, is not his: I hope folks don’t find this approach too offputting: it was the pleasantest, least obstrusive I could find.

The reader is invited to download these .mp3 files, save them to a memory stick and listen to them. That’s the whole idea.
